About Me

Geek. Sysadmin. Terribly eclectic music taste.

I have been working as a Sysadmin for 10+ years. I have been working with Linux for most of that time and truly believe in the Unix philosophies of system design.

Having said that, I am not a 'Linux fanboy' and I am very excited by the direction Microsoft is taking Windows Server (Powershell, Core, etc).

I specialise in Automation, Virtualisation, and DevOps. I see DevOps as a methodology (not a Job Title) and I believe that automation & configuration management improve efficiency, provide stability, and manage security.

This site is "not a blog"; it is simply an area to collect my scripts, hacks and notes.
