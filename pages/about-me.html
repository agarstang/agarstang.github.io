About Me

Geek. Linux enthusiast. Terribly eclectic music taste.

I have been working as a Sysadmin for 8+ years. I have been working with Linux for most of that time and truly believe in the Unix philosophies of system design.

Having said that, I am not a 'Linux fanboy' and I am very excited by the direction Microsoft is taking Windows Server (Powershell, Core, O365 etc).

I am currently spending most of my time working with Ubuntu, Puppet, Vagrant (for testing), and VMWare ESXi. I work for 8Networks the IT Services company behind Elastic Desktop.

This site is "not a blog"; it is simply an area to correlate my scripts, hacks and notes.
